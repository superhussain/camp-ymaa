Privacy Policy

Information about our organization and website

YMAA respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. We are providing this Privacy Policy to inform you of our Privacy Policy and practices and of the choices you can make about the way your information is collected online and how that information is used. We hope that this will remove any concerns you may have about supplying information to us and make visiting Camp YMAA website a rewarding experience. By accessing and browsing this website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully and revisit this page from time to time to review any changes that may have been made.

Providing visitors with anonymous access

Initially, please note that you can access our website home pages and browse each of our sites without disclosing your personal data. Also, our website do not enable our visitors to communicate with other visitors or to post information to be accessed by other visitors to our website.

Sites covered by this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all YMAA owned website and domains, including thebestcampever.ca. The Camp YMAA website may provide links to third-party website for your convenience and information. If you access those links, you will leave the Camp YMAA website. We do not control those sites or their privacy practices, which may differ from our own privacy practices. This Privacy Policy does not cover any personal data that you choose to give to unrelated third parties. You acknowledge that we do not monitor or control the information collected by such sites or the privacy practices of any third parties, and we are not responsible for their practices or the content of their sites.

Types of information we collect

When browsing any of the Camp YMAA website, you are not required to provide any personal information. If you choose to register with the Camp YMAA website, then you will be requested to provide contact information (name, address, telephone number, email address). We use this information to contact you about the services on our site in which you have expressed interest. We may also use your contact information to send you information about other YMAA products and services as well as promotional material that may be of interest to you from some of our marketing partners. If you do not want to receive these promotional announcements, you may opt-out when you provide us with contact information or at any time after you register by updating your preferences (see the Opt-Out section below).

The information is used by YMAA to process your orders, enable participation in promotions, and facilitate your relationship with us. Through the Camp YMAA website, you can order products or services, request information, or subscribe to marketing or support materials. In order to purchase a product or service from us, we will request certain personally identifiable information from you. You will need to provide contact information (such as name, email and shipping address) and financial information (such as credit card number, billing address, and expiration date). Financial information collected from you is used only to bill you for the products and services that you have purchased.

Communications from the Camp YMAA Website

Once you register, we will respond to your inquiries, provide services you request and manage your account. We will communicate with you by email or telephone and will make every effort to honor your preference. We will occasionally send you information on new products, services, special savings, promotions and general information. If you do not want to receive this information, you can opt-out of receiving future mailings (see Opt-Out section below). By providing your personal data to the Camp YMAA website, you are acknowledging and agreeing that any of your personal data the YMAA may be transferred across state borders and country borders (including outside the EEA) for the purposes of data consolidation, storage, simplified customer information management, reporting and other internal uses. YMAAmay process, disclose or transfer such personal data and in doing so, such personal data may be transferred across state borders or country borders (including outside the EEA). In addition, such personal data may be transmitted to other affiliates within YMAA. Non-personal data is aggregated for reporting about the Camp YMAA website usability, performance and effectiveness. It is used to improve the customer experience, usability and site content. Pages and content may be changed based on feedback from visitors.

Credit card information is used only for payment processing and fraud prevention. Credit card information and sensitive personal data are not used for any other purpose by YMAA and will not be kept longer than necessary for providing the services, unless you ask us to retain your credit card information for future purchases. Please note that we do not provide your email address to our business partners. However, we may send you offers on behalf of our business partners. You can opt out from receiving email offers from YMAA as described below. In the event that YMAA is sold or acquired, customer information will be one of the assets transferred. In addition, we reserve the right to disclose information submitted by or concerning any user of this Camp YMAA website as required by law or when we reasonably believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our systems, our business or the rights of others.

Sharing of information

YMAA contracts with third-party service providers and suppliers to deliver complete products, services and customer solutions described above. These service providers may change or we may contract with additional service providers to better accommodate our customers. YMAA will not share personal information with any other third parties without your permission, unless required by law enforcement action, subpoena, local law or when we reasonably believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our systems, our business or the rights of others or as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy.

How we collect information

The Camp YMAA Website collects data through a variety of different ways. For instance, we automatically log data by means of programming and we may link certain information as well. We also collect data through forms that allow users to request information about YMAA and its services.

Camp YMAA website may collect non-personal technical information about your session (such as browser type, Internet service provider, platform type, internet protocol (IP) addresses, referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp). We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, diagnose problems with our server and administer the Camp YMAA Website, to track user movement and use, and to gather broad demographic information. One way we gather this information is through the use of cookies; we use cookies to store personal data and link information. Cookies are small computer files stored on your computer’s hard drive that save and retrieve information about your visit to the Camp YMAA Website. If you are uncomfortable regarding the use of cookies, you may disable cookies on your computer by changing the settings in the preferences or options menu in your browser. However, if you choose to disable cookies, you may not be able to access certain areas of the Camp YMAA Website.

Children’s privacy

YMAA is committed to protecting the privacy needs of children and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children’s online activities and interests. YMAA does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 and YMAA does not target its website to children under 13.

Opting-out

YMAA provides you the opportunity to “opt-out” of having your personally identifiable information used for certain purposes when we ask for this information. For example, if you purchase a product from us but do not wish to receive any additional marketing material from us, you can indicate your preference on the order form. If you no longer wish to receive [our newsletters and/or] promotional communications, you may opt-out of receiving them by emailing us at ymaacamp@gmail.com.

Access to and accuracy of your information

We will provide you with access to your information, including making reasonable effort to provide you with online access and the opportunity to change your information. To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity, such as a password and user ID, before granting access to your data. We reserve the right to refuse to provide our visitors with a copy of their personal data, but will give reasons for our refusal.

Keeping your information secure

The security of your personal information is important to us; however, no security system is guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, YMAA cannot guarantee or warrant the security of our servers nor can we guarantee that information you supply through the Camp YMAA Website will not be intercepted while being transmitted over the Internet. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. When you enter sensitive information (such as credit card information) on our registration or order forms, we encrypt that information using secured socket layer technology.

Business transitions

In the event that YMAA goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your personally identifiable information will likely be among the assets transferred.
